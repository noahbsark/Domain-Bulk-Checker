      FAQ
      
      
      
        Are the results guaranteed?
        
      No. Treat this as a fast triage tool. A registrar can still mark a domain premium, reserved, blocked, or unavailable.
      
      
      
      
      
        Why does it say “possibly available”?
        
      Public RDAP/DNS checks suggest the name may not be registered. The app cannot complete a live checkout or reserve the domain.
      
      
      
      
      
        What does the quality score mean?
        
      It is a heuristic ranking based on length, TLD, keywords, clarity, brandability, buyer intent, word order, and penalties. It is not a market valuation.
      
      
      
      
      
        Can I check thousands of domains?
        
      Yes, but go slowly. Public RDAP/DNS services may rate-limit requests. For very large lists, use score-only mode first and check availability only for the shortlist.
      
      
      
      
      
        Do you store my domain list?
        
      No backend database is used in this static app. Saved sessions are stored in your browser local storage unless you clear them.
      
      
      
      
      
        Are registrar links affiliate links?
        
      Some may be. If affiliate links are enabled, the site may earn a commission at no extra cost to you. The registrar still controls final checkout, price, and availability.
