Important limitations

This is a static browser app with no registrar API key and no backend. RDAP/DNS results are useful for filtering, but possibly available means “worth checking,” not “guaranteed purchasable.” Confirm availability and price at a registrar before buying.

Privacy: pasted lists and saved sessions stay in this browser's local storage. Availability checks still contact public RDAP/DNS services, and registrar buttons open external sites.

FAQ

Are the results guaranteed?

No. Treat this as a fast triage tool. A registrar can still mark a domain premium, reserved, blocked, or unavailable.

Why does it say “possibly available”?

Public RDAP/DNS checks suggest the name may not be registered. The app cannot complete a live checkout or reserve the domain.

What does the quality score mean?

It is a heuristic ranking based on length, TLD, keywords, clarity, brandability, buyer intent, word order, and penalties. It is not a market valuation.

Can I check thousands of domains?

Yes, but go slowly. Public RDAP/DNS services may rate-limit requests. The table renders in chunks to reduce browser lag.
