Owner launch checklist

Keep launch simple.

Use this page before sharing the app publicly. It is safe to keep in the repo, marked noindex, and linked from owner mode only.

Back to owner mode Inputs needed Custom domain setup

1. Public basics

  • Choose the final public site name and domain.
  • Update canonical URL, Open Graph URL, sitemap, and public constants together.
  • Confirm privacy, terms, and affiliate disclosure pages open.
  • Run owner preflight from index.html?owner=1.
  • Confirm public launch mode is on.
  • Clean results view: All Results appears automatically after checking.
  • Compact results view: hero/input collapse after checking and All Results is easy to scan.
  • Results polish: test Compact/Comfortable All Results view and save/remove feedback.

2. Main user flow

  • Paste a short sample list.
  • Confirm Top Picks show simply.
  • Save 2–5 finalists.
  • Pick a winner, mark it checked, and confirm Winner ready appears.
  • Click Done and confirm it copies the winner report.

3. Price links

  • Click Check best choice and saved price links.
  • Test Copy unchecked links and Open unchecked links.
  • Confirm links go to the expected registrar search page.
  • Do not claim live price comparison unless a real pricing API is added.

4. Archive manager

  • Enable Advanced Mode.
  • Click Archive session.
  • Confirm the archive appears under Local archives.
  • Test Restore, Download JSON, Delete, and Clear all archives.
  • Confirm archive data stays local in the browser only.

5. Privacy and analytics

  • Keep analytics disabled unless a safe endpoint is configured.
  • Do not track pasted domains, uploaded files, saved names, notes, or archives.
  • Test on phone and desktop.
