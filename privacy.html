Privacy

Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-16. Replace this date and wording with your final policy before a public launch.

Plain-English summary: Domain Shortlist is designed as a static browser tool. It does not require an account and does not intentionally collect or sell your pasted domain list.

Information you enter

You may paste domain names, URLs, keywords, or upload TXT/CSV files. The app processes that information in your browser to score and organize results.

Local storage

If you save or restore a session, the data is stored in your browser's local storage on your device. You can clear it from inside the app or through your browser settings.

Availability checks

If you run live availability checks, your browser may contact public RDAP or DNS services to estimate whether a domain may be available. These third-party services may receive the domain being checked.

Registrar links

When you click a registrar button, you leave this site and the registrar handles final search, pricing, availability, checkout, privacy, and payment.

Analytics

This package does not include analytics by default. If analytics are added later, avoid sending pasted domain names or file contents to analytics tools.

Contact and feedback

To report a bad result or privacy concern, use the feedback link in the app footer or open an issue on the project repository.
